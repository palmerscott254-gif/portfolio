# Technical Blog Notes

## Durable Product Systems
Durable systems are designed for handoff across generations of maintainers. They prioritize explicit boundaries, operational runbooks, and reversible change.

## Human-Centered AI Systems
AI should improve decision quality rather than obscure accountability. Every model-assisted recommendation needs a traceable rationale.

## Performance Culture
Performance is a product feature. Prioritize interaction latency, backend p95 targets, and build-time budgets as first-class acceptance criteria.
