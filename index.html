About Me

I'm a Kenyan-born computer science and design professional with a passion for creating digital experiences that blend innovation with *cultural authenticity*. My journey started in the vibrant tech scene of Nairobi and has evolved into a global perspective on design and development.

I thrive at the intersection of code and creativity, bringing a unique African perspective to the global design community while maintaining world-class standards. My work draws inspiration from traditional African aesthetics, such as *geometric patterns and vibrant color theory*, combined with cutting-edge technology.

When I'm not coding or designing, I'm exploring emerging technologies, mentoring other young African talents, and working on projects that can make a positive impact in my community and beyond.
